Bio

I grew up in Castlemaine, VIC, Australia. I did a BA/BSc at The University of Melbourne, triple-majoring in Genetics, Psychology, and the History & Philosophy of Science. I did a MS in Machine Learning, and an MS and PhD in Logic, Computation and Methodology at Carnegie Mellon University. I moved back to Melbourne in 2017 for a postdoc, and have now transitioned to industry.
